FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
January 24, 2018
From: Ben Chapman for State Senate

On January 13th, Ben Chapman declared his write-in candidacy for the Illinois 51st State Senate seat. This will be the first time Senator Chapin Rose has been challenged by a Democrat as Senator.

In announcing his run, Chapman said “The Illinois 51st deserves to be represented by someone who listens to their constituents and acts in their service - not just in the service of their party leadership. I look forward to discussing my forward-looking agenda and demonstrating how it will work for everyone in Illinois.”

“Issues such as electoral reform, public health advancements, profitable and sustainable agriculture, expanded educational opportunities, and clean air and water are issues that habitants of IL 51 care deeply about, but they are not yet being addressed sufficiently at the state level. I will change that. Part of that effort requires calming the fires of political polarization in our state and working with both sides of the aisle. This means listening to all the voices in the room.”

Ben Chapman is a junior at the University of Illinois studying Food Science and Human Nutrition. He is the co-Chair of the Political Action Committee of the Students for Environmental Concerns and is a writer and contributor to local media outlets. While he is a full-time student, he lives in Mahomet and has lived there his whole life. Chapman is currently a write-in candidate, but he will eventually seek party appointment to the November ballot.
